ABOUT SUPERCELL

Supercell, Finnish mobile game giant. It has global popular games such as tribal conflict, cartoon farm, Island Raiders, Royal war and wild fighting.

By March 2020, supercell had launched five games for the global market: tribal conflict, cartoon farm, Island Raiders, Royal war and wild fighting. In contrast, Zynga, which has more mobile game products, has about 21 million daily active users, while Gameloft and Glu have about 19 million and 5 million users respectively. King, the developer of candy legend, has more than 130 million daily active users.
